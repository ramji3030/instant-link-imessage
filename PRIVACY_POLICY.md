PRIVACY_POLICY.md# Privacy Policy for InstantLink

**Last Updated:** February 28, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to InstantLink. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iMessage social networking application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Profile Information**: Username, email address (.edu for verification)
- **Contact Information**: Access to contacts (with your permission) to find friends
- **Photos & Media**: Images you share through instant connections
- **Messages**: Content shared via iMessage extension

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: App interactions, feature usage, timestamps
- **Device Information**: iOS version, device model, unique identifiers
- **Location Data**: General location for matching algorithm (if enabled)

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Providing Services**: Enable instant connections and messaging
2. **AI Matching**: Recommend connections using k-means clustering
3. **Personalization**: Improve user experience and content
4. **Communication**: Send notifications and updates
5. **Analytics**: Understand usage patterns and improve app
6. **Safety & Security**: Detect fraud, prevent abuse

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share information with:
- **Service Providers**: Cloud hosting (AWS), analytics
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law
- **Your Connections**: Information you choose to share

## Data Security

- End-to-end encryption for messages
- Secure HTTPS connections
- Regular security audits
- Limited employee access to data

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request data deletion
- Opt-out of data collection
- Export your data
- Withdraw consent

## Children's Privacy

InstantLink requires users to be 18+ or have .edu email verification. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Data Retention

- Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
- Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days
- Legal obligations: Some data may be retained longer

## Third-Party Services

- **OpenGraph**: Link preview scraping
- **Socket.io**: Real-time messaging
- **AWS/PostgreSQL**: Data storage

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. You'll be notified of significant changes via app notification or email.
